Viewing HTML source of a webpage in Google Chrome

  1. Open Google Chrome.
  2. Navigate to the webpage you wish to look at.
  3. Right click on the element you wish to examine.
  4. Select "inspect." A window will appear at the right of the screen
    containing the HTML source for the webpage, with the code of the element
    you selected highlighted.
  5. This action shall henceforth be known as "peeking behind the tapestry,"
    because it allows us to take what we see (the tapestry's exterior design/website)
    and pick a specific part of the fabric (element of the page) and look
    at the back of it to see how it was stitched together.
